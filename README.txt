** INSTALLATION **

1) You'll need a webserver for this. Any webhost that has

the most recent version PHP will do just fine.

2) Extract the "smoothLoad" folder onto your web server.

3) Set your configurations in `config/config.js`.

4) Add sv_loadingurl "http://yourwebsite.com/BubbleLoad/?steamid=%s" to your server.cfg.

5) Type exec server.cfg into your console or restart your server.

6) You're done!
